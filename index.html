Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

WriteAssist AI ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that provides grammar checking, text rewriting, diagram generation, and cover letter creation services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Text Data: When you use WriteAssist AI, the following text data may be processed:

  • Text you input or select for grammar checking
  • Text you input or select for rewriting
  • Prompts you provide for diagram generation
  • Information you enter in the cover letter generator

Technical Data: We may collect your language preference settings and per-site enable/disable preferences stored locally in your browser.

How We Use Your Information

Text you submit is sent to our backend servers (hosted on Supabase) for processing. Our AI services analyze and process your text to provide grammar corrections, rewrites, diagrams, or cover letters.

We do not store your text data permanently - it is processed in real-time and not retained after the response is generated.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your text data with advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing the extension's services

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider. Supabase processes your text data according to their Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Our backend services use secure authentication
  • We do not store sensitive personal information

Permissions Explained

Permission Purpose
storage Save your language and site preferences locally
activeTab Access the current tab to enable/disable the extension
scripting Inject the inline WriteAssist panel on supported sites
downloads Download generated diagrams (PNG) and cover letters (TXT)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable the extension on any website using the site toggle
  • Clear your local preferences by removing the extension
  • Stop using the service at any time by uninstalling the extension

Data Retention

  • Text data: Not retained - processed in real-time only
  • Local preferences: Stored until you clear browser data or uninstall

Children's Privacy

WriteAssist AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
